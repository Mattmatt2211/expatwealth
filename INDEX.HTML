About Us

ExpatWealth was founded with the mission to empower global citizens to take control of their finances while living abroad. We understand the challenges of managing money across borders — different currencies, banking systems, and spending habits — and we built a tool to make it simple, secure, and stress-free.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is ExpatWealth free to use?

Yes! We offer a free plan with essential features to help you manage your finances overseas. For power users, our Pro plan includes advanced tools and insights.

Which currencies do you support?

We support over 100 currencies including USD, EUR, GBP, JPY, AUD, and many more.

Can I use this while traveling?

Absolutely. ExpatWealth is designed for expats, nomads, and frequent travelers who need real-time insights on the go.
